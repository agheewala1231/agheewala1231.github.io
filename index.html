Kobe Bryant's Farewell

Kobe Bryant was on fire for his final NBA game, giving the Los Angeles Lakers one more amazing show of offence to close out his 20-year career. Scoring 60 points out off 50 shots Bryant brought his playing career to an end on Wednesday night with the Lakers beating the Utah Jazz 101-96. It was a storybook finish, the sixth 60-point game of his career. It also was the most shots taken by anyone in an NBA game in the past 30 seasons. He retires as the NBA's third-highest all-time scorer and won five championships.
